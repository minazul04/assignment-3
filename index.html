Program Sections

At Icebreakers, we're passionate about hockey and dedicated to providing an outstanding experience for players of all ages and skill levels. Our program is designed to foster athletic skill, teamwork, and a love for the game in a fun and competitive environment.

Junior Program

In our Hockey Academy, a fun and exciting introduction to the world of hockey for our youngest players! Our junior program is designed to ignite a passion for the game in a safe and supportive environment.

Senior Program

Our Next Gen Hockey Stars program is tailored for teenagers, blending skill development with competitive play. This program is perfect for those who want to take their game to the next level while enjoying the spirit of youth hockey.

Professional Program Program

Elite Hockey Prospects is our program for aspiring professional players and serious enthusiasts. This rigorous program is designed to refine skills, enhance strategic understanding, and prepare players for high-level competition.
